Facilities & Activities

Everything you need for the lifestyle you want

Community Hub

The Lodge

Our beautiful lodge serves as the social and dining heart of Highfield Country Estate. With full service restaurant and dining facilities, comfortable lounge and sitting areas, event spaces for gatherings and celebrations, a library, reading rooms, and wellness and leisure facilities, The Lodge has everything you need.

The Lodge is designed as a welcoming community hub where residents come together to dine, socialise, and create lasting memories.

Outdoor Living

Beautifully Landscaped Gardens

Our extensive grounds feature mature gardens, walking paths, and peaceful outdoor spaces. Our dedicated garden team maintains the property to the highest standard year-round.

Whether you enjoy walking, sitting beneath the trees, or participating in gardening activities, our gardens provide an ideal setting for outdoor living and relaxation in the beautiful Te Awamutu climate.

Stay Active

Active Lifestyle & Community

Bowls

Our well-maintained bowling green is a popular gathering place for residents. Whether you're an experienced player or a beginner, there's always room for friendly competition and socialising.

Social Events

Regular social gatherings, entertainment events, and celebrations create opportunities to build friendships and enjoy the company of fellow residents in a relaxed atmosphere.

Woodworking Workshop

Our fully equipped woodworking workshop is a favourite among residents. With quality tools and workbenches, it's the perfect place to craft, create, and share skills with fellow enthusiasts.

Games & Recreation

Snooker, billiards, card games, and more — our recreation rooms offer the perfect setting for friendly competition and relaxation with fellow residents.

Hobby Groups

Music, crafts, and other hobbies bring residents together. Whether you play an instrument or want to learn, our hobby groups offer a welcoming space to share your passions.

Wellness

Health and wellness activities including walking groups, fitness classes, and wellness sessions support your wellbeing throughout the year.

Out & About

Weekly Van Trips & Outings

Our village van makes regular outings every week, so you can stay connected with the town and enjoy time out with fellow residents — no driving required.

Our Location

Located in Beautiful Te Awamutu

Highfield Country Estate is perfectly positioned to enjoy the best of Te Awamutu, a thriving provincial town with excellent amenities, attractions, and a strong community spirit.

Local Attractions

Te Awamutu Museum and Historical Society, meaningful art galleries and cultural venues, shopping and dining at Regent Street, parks and recreational areas, and easy access to Te Pehukaroa Domain.

Why Te Awamutu

Strong sense of community, excellent healthcare facilities, beautiful natural scenery, a central location with easy access to Hamilton and beyond, and a safe, welcoming town to call home.
