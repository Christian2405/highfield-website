Our Villas

Premium Living

Luxury Villas for Every Lifestyle

Each villa at Highfield Country Estate is thoughtfully designed with modern living in mind. Our collection features spacious floor plans ranging from 112sqm to 172sqm, with a variety of configurations to suit different needs.

Every villa includes quality finishes, modern appliances, outdoor living spaces, and accessibility features that make retirement living comfortable and convenient.

Attention to Detail

Quality Design & Comfort

Our villas feature modern kitchens with quality appliances, spacious living and dining areas, comfortable bedrooms with built-in storage, accessible bathrooms with contemporary fixtures, private outdoor terraces and gardens, and undercover parking or garaging options.

Every detail has been considered to ensure your villa feels like a true home from day one.

Standard Inclusions

Every Villa Includes

Quality Interiors

Modern fitted kitchens with quality appliances, spacious cupboard storage, contemporary bathrooms, and excellent insulation for year-round comfort.

Outdoor Living

Private terraces and gardens designed for entertaining or peaceful relaxation. Many plans include potential for additional garden development.

Accessibility

Thoughtful design with accessibility in mind, including single-level living, accessible bathrooms, and easy movement throughout.

Parking

Safe and convenient parking options, including undercover or garaged options to protect your vehicle.
