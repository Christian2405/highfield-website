Heart of the Estate

The Lodge

Our beautifully appointed lodge is the heart of Highfield Country Estate. With comfortable dining facilities, lounge areas, and spaces for entertainment, The Lodge provides the perfect setting for both relaxation and socialising.

Whether you're enjoying afternoon tea, attending a hosted event, or simply taking in the peaceful surroundings, The Lodge creates a welcoming atmosphere for all our residents.

Natural Beauty

Our Gardens

Set within beautifully landscaped grounds, Highfield's gardens offer tranquil spaces for walking, reflection, and enjoying nature. Our team works tirelessly to maintain the grounds, creating seasonal beauty throughout the year.

From established trees to carefully planted flower beds, every corner of our gardens has been designed to provide peace and inspiration for our residents and their visitors.

Community Life

Activities & Community

From bowls and social gatherings to cultural events and outdoor activities, there's always something happening at Highfield. Our active community embraces new experiences and values the friendships formed here.

Whether you're a keen bowler, enjoy card games, or prefer quiet hobbies, our lifestyle program is designed to suit your interests and keep you engaged with the Highfield community.

Designed for Living

Modern Comfort Meets Thoughtful Design

Our villas are purpose-built for retirement living, combining contemporary design with accessibility and comfort. Each villa is thoughtfully planned to maximise your living space and quality of life.

With a range of floor plans from 112sqm to 172sqm, there's a villa perfect for your lifestyle. Features include modern kitchens, spacious living areas, and outdoor terraces to enjoy the beautiful Te Awamutu scenery.
