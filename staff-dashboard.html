Awards

Shown in the “Award-Winning Village” section on the home page. Add a certificate image address to display the certificate; leave it blank for a text card. New awards appear at the top, ready to fill in.

Testimonials

Quotes shown on the Testimonials page, in the order below.

Team

The “Meet Our Team” section on the Testimonials page. Drag order with the arrows; visitors click a member to reveal their bio.

News

Articles on the News page. Newest first — new articles are added to the top. Use the arrows to reorder.

Photos

Photos on the Gallery page, organised into sections. Use each section’s “Add Photo” button — the new photo appears at the top of that section.

Trips & Events

Shown in the “Weekly Van Trips & Outings” section on the Facilities page. The day appears as a badge on each card.

Available Villas

Villas listed for sale on the Our Villas page. When this list is empty, the “Available Villas” section is hidden from the website automatically.
